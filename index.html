Animal House Shelter is a non-profit, no-kill
501[c]3 Shelter for all breeds of dogs and Cats.
We rescues, care for, and find homes for
homeless pets who arrive at AHS for various
reasons, including:

  • Abuse, neglect, or abandonment
  • Owners Whose time, income, or situation changes
  • Animal Scheduled for euthanasia at other clinics

Once rehabilitated and ready, they are carefully matched with homes for
adoption or foster care. Will their new home be yours? Apply to Adopt!
